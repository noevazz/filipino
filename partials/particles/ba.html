Ba

The ba particle is used to turn a sentence into a yes-no question.

It usually comes after the first important word or subject.

Ito ay iyong libro/This is your book
Ito ba ay iyong libro?/Is this your book?
Kumain ka/You ate
Kumain ka ba?/Did you eat?
Ito ay kotse ko/This is my car
Ito ba ay kotse ko?/is this my car?
Si Andrea ang guro/Andrea is the teache
Si Andrea ba ang guro?/is Andrea the teacher?
