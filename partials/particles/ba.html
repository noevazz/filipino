Understanding the Filipino Particle "ba"

The ba particle is used to turn a sentence into a yes-no question.

It usually comes after the first important word or subject.

Usage

Add ba after the adjective to make it a question:

Masarap!/Delicious!
Masarap ba?/Delicious?
Totoo!/It is true!
Totoo ba?/Is it true?

Add ba between the adjective and two syllable personal pronoun:

Maganda siya/She is beautiful
Maganda ba siya?/Is she beautiful?

Add ba between the verb and two syllable personal pronoun:

Kakain ako/I will eat
Kakain ba ako?/Will I eat?

Add ba after the monosyllable personal pronoun:

Nanaloka ka!/You won!
Nanaloka ka ba?/Did you win?
Kumain ka/You ate
Kumain ka ba?/Did you eat?

Add ba between the proper noun and the demonstrative pronoun to make it a question:

Si Noe iyon/It is Now
Si Noe ba iyon?/Is that Noe?

Add ba between the adjective and the demonstrative pronoun:

Tama ito/It is correct
Tama ba ito?/Is it correct?
