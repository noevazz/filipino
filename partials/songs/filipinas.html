Filipinas

About the song

​The song "Filipinas" by Trío Los Panchos is a heartfelt tribute to the Philippines, composed during the group's visit to Manila in 1958. It was featured on their album Siete Notas de Amor, which includes Spanish renditions of Filipino folk songs.

This particular track, written by Chucho Navarro, expresses deep admiration and affection for the Philippines, referring to the country as a beloved mother, an enchanting sweetheart, and the "princess of the Orient."

The lyrics highlight the nation's warmth, respect, and the devotion of its people, who are portrayed as offering the "blood of their heart" to their homeland.
