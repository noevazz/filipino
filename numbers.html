For numbers 11 to 19 it follows a pattern:

  1. Labing-isa
  2. Labindalawa
  3. labintatlo
  4. labing-apat
  5. labinlima
  6. labing-anim
  7. labimpito
  8. labingwalo
  9. labinsiyam
The rule is:

labi + connector + number from 1-9

The connectors can be:

  • na: when the previous letter is consonant.
  • ng: when the previous letter is a vowel.
Since labi ends with i we use ng, but this connector change depending on the next letter:
  • if the next letter is d, t, l, s the ng connector change to n to make it easier to pronounce.
  • If p follows the ng connector then the ng becomes m
