Pronouns Sa Group

Person Filipino Pronoun English Translation
1st Person Singular Sa akin To me, for me
2nd Person Singular Sa iyo To you, for you
3rd Person Singular Sa kanya To him/her, for him/her
1st Person Plural Sa atin To us (inclusive), for us (inclusive)
1st Person Plural Sa amin To us (exclusive), for us (exclusive)
2nd Person Plural Sa inyo To you all, for you all
3rd Person Plural Sa kanila To them, for them

Pronouns in the Sa group has 4 functions:

Function Example Sentence Meaning
Direction Pupunta ako sa iyo. I will go to you.
Location Nasa kanya ang susi. The key is with him/her.
Recipient Nagbigay ako ng regalo sa kanila. I gave a gift to them.
Possession Ang sasakyan sa tatay ko. My dad's car.
