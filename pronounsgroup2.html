Pronouns Ng Group

Person Filipino Pronoun English Translation
1st Person Singular Ko My
2nd Person Singular Mo Your
3rd Person Singular Niya His/Her
1st Person Plural Natin Our (inclusive)
1st Person Plural Namin Our (exclusive)
2nd Person Plural Ninyo Your (plural/polite)
3rd Person Plural Nila Their

The ng group is used for possession or to indicate an object that is being affected by the action. It is often used when the pronoun is part of the object of the sentence.

Example:

Libro ko / My book

Notes:

  • Pronouns in the NG group cannot be used at the beginning of a sentence.
